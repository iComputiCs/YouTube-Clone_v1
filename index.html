Keyboard shortcuts:

"Spacebar" - To Play and Pause the video

"n" - To skip to Next video

"p" - To go back to the Previous video

"Right Arrow" - To go 5 seconds forward

"Left Arrow" - To go 5 seconds backward

"m" - To Mute the video

"a" - To turn on/off Autoplay

"r" - To Refresh and randomize the order of the videos

"s" - To focus to the Search input

"c" - To focus to the Comment input

"f" - To make the video fullscreen, or to exit from that
